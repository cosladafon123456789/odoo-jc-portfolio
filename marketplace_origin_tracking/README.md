# Marketplace & Sales Origin Tracking

Sales origin tracking in Odoo 17 for multi-channel eCommerce operations.

## Business Problem
Companies selling across multiple channels (Amazon, BackMarket, Web, B2B, etc.)
often lack clear visibility on:
- where sales originate
- profitability per channel
- operational impact per marketplace

This limits decision-making and margin optimization.

## Solution
Implement origin tracking at Sales Order and Product level, enabling
channel-based reporting and automation.

## Key Features
- Sales origin field (Amazon, BackMarket, Web, B2B, etc.)
- Automatic origin assignment
- Channel-based reporting and KPIs
- Rules and automations per origin (pricing, logistics, commissions)

## Business Impact
- Clear profitability by channel
- Better strategic decisions
- Optimized marketplace operations
- Improved margin control

## Odoo Version
- Odoo 17 Enterprise

## Tech
- Custom fields and compute logic
- Domain filters and smart buttons
- Reporting-ready data model
