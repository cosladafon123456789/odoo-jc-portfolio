# REBU VAT Margin & Stock Flow

Custom Odoo 17 logic to manage second-hand goods under VAT margin (REBU) regime.

## Business Problem
Standard Odoo flows do not correctly handle VAT margin requirements for refurbished and second-hand products.

Manual calculations increase the risk of legal errors and incorrect margins.

## Solution
Custom stock and accounting logic designed to support REBU operations end-to-end.

## Key Features
- REBU-compliant stock flows
- Margin-based VAT calculations
- Clear traceability per device
- Audit-ready structure

## Business Impact
- Legal compliance
- Accurate profit calculation
- Reduced accounting risk

## Odoo Version
- Odoo 17 Enterprise
